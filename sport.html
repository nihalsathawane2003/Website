About Department

The Department of Computer Engineering was established in 1995 with an intake capacity of 60 students. The main objective was to start B.Tech in Computer Engineering under this University to provide technical and professional manpower to the IT Industry. The department aims to provide opportunities to the young and talented minds of the Kokan region, state of Maharashtra. In 2001, the department started the M.Tech program in Computer Engineering with an intake capacity of 18, and in 2007, the PhD program was also introduced.

Department Vision

The department is committed to achieving academic excellence through education and research in the emerging and established areas of computer science and engineering.

Department Mission

Our mission is to build the capacities of young students to realize their professional and academic dreams.

Department Goal

Our goal is to provide state-of-the-art courses in the discipline of Computer Science and Engineering.

University Strategic Plan

  • Openness: The University is open to all and embraces diversity, respecting the perspectives and contributions of students, faculty, staff, and society.
  • Integrity: We strive to be consistently honest and fair in our dealings, respecting each other's interests and abilities to work together towards excellence.
  • Innovation: We aim to innovate and find local solutions to societal problems, while upholding academic rigor and global reach.
  • Commitment: The faculty, students, and staff are committed to providing quality technical education and becoming a 'Centre of Excellence' in engineering education.
  • Success in Societal Relevance: We strive to respond to the needs of our stakeholders in a helpful, timely, and sensitive manner, applying creative solutions in a rapidly changing technology landscape.
