About Department

The Department of Computer Engineering is established in 1995 with an intake capacity of 60 students. The main objective was to start B. Tech in Computer Engineering under this University to provide technical and professional manpower to the IT Industry. Furthermore, the University will provide an opportunity to the young and talented minds of the Kokan region, state of Maharashtra. Later, in the year 2001, the Department of Computer Engineering started M. Tech in Computer Engineering with an intake capacity of 18 and also started a PhD program in the year 2007. Now, as per the Sections 3 (3) and 4 (1) of Chapter II of the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University Act, XXIX 2014, the University is an affiliating technical University and the territorial limits, within which the powers conferred upon the University by this Act, shall comprise the whole of Maharashtra, and it may affiliate any college or institution conducting engineering, pharmacy, architecture, hotel management, and catering technology courses for the conferment of degrees, diplomas, or grant certificates to the students admitted therein.
